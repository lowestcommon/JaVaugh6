Class

Here we have the 12 main classes in DND 5e

Barbarian

A physical attacker who battles with strong emotions like rage. This class is exceptionally bulky and can shake off many attacks.

Bard

A magical support who uses the magic found in art. This class is skilled in inspiring their team.

Cleric

A magical support who believes in higher powers in the worlds. This class makes great healers and attackers.

Druid

A magical support who harnesses the powers in nature. This class can shape the environment and themselves.

Fighter

A physical attacker who battles with weapons, armor, and tactics. This class is great at most things relating to combat.

Monk

A physical attacker who hones their body as a weapon. This class can enhance their punches and kicks with energy.

Paladin

A physical and magical attacker who belivees in a higher cause. This class can attack, defend, heal, use spells, and is naturally influential.

Ranger

A physical and magical attacker who studies the land and their enemies. This class can track targets down and lock onto them for extra damage.

Rouge

A physical support who uses their expertise to bend luck. This class is can disappear before making extremly leathal attacks.

Sorcerer

A magical attacker who is born with magical within them. This class can modify their magic with unique effects.

Warlock

A magical attacker who makes a deal with a greater entity for magic. This class can use uncanny abilities that don't belong to them.

Wizard

A magical attacker who studies magic in ancient books and old libraries. This class has access to nearly every magical ability in the game.
