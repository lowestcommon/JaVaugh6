Here we have the 12 main classes in DND 5e

Class determines what abilities and what skills you have access to

Each class has different strengths, weaknesses, and stats that they use.

Class Description Stat
Barbarian A physical attacker who battles with strong emotions like rage. This class is exceptionally bulky and can shake off many attacks. Strength and constitution
Bard A magical support who uses the magic found in art. This class is skilled in inspiring their team. Charisma
Cleric A magical support who believes in higher powers in the worlds. This class makes great healers and attackers. Wisdom
Druid A magical support who harnesses the powers in nature. This class can shape the environment and themselves. Wisdom
Fighter A physical attacker who battles with weapons, armor, and tactics. This class is great at most things relating to combat. Strength or dexterity and constitution
Monk A physical attacker who hones their body as a weapon. This class can enhance their punches and kicks with energy. Dexterity and wisdom
Paladin A physical and magical attacker who belivees in a higher cause. This class can attack, defend, heal, use spells, and is naturally influential. Strength and charisma
Ranger A physical and magical attacker who studies the land and their enemies. This class can track targets down and lock onto them for extra damage. Dexterity and wisdom
Rouge A physical support who uses their expertise to bend luck. This class is can disappear before making extremly leathal attacks. Dexterity
Sorcerer A magical attacker who is born with magical within them. This class can modify their magic with unique effects. Charisma
Warlock A magical attacker who makes a deal with a greater entity for magic. This class can use uncanny abilities that don't belong to them. Charisma
Wizard A magical attacker who studies magic in ancient books and old libraries. This class has access to nearly every magical ability in the game. Intelligence
