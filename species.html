Species

Here we have the 10 main classes in DND 5e

Aasimar

An enlighting species born from angels and gods. This species can heal and naturally light places up.

Dragonborn

A proud species born from dragons. This species can breath fire or any element that their associated dragon could breath.

Dwarf

A sturdy species born underground. This species is durable like the stone they come from.

Elf

An elegant species born in a distant world. This species follows has keen senses and strong curiosity.

Gnome

A small species born to tinker. This species has a knack for invention.

Goliath

A tall species born from giants. This species is large and lives in mountains.

Halfling

A humble species born comforable retired. This small species is favored by luck.

Human

A common species born to populate. This species is good at anything they put themselves to do.

Orc

A strong species born in strife. This species is built with ultimate endurance.

Tiefling

An infernal species born from lower planes. This species has devil horns but can choose to be good.
