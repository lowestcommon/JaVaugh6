Here we have the 10 main species in DND 5e

Species determines how your character looks and what traits they have.

Each species has different abilities and characteristics to consider.

While each species looks different, each has the ability to choose between good or evil.

Species Description Abilities
Aasimar An enlighting species born from angels and gods. This species can heal and naturally light places up. Healing and light spells
Dragonborn A proud species born from dragons. This species can breath fire or any element that their associated dragon could breath. Dragon breath
Dwarf A sturdy species born underground. This species is durable like the stone they come from. Resilience and toughness
Elf An elegant species born in a distant world. This species follows has keen senses and strong curiosity. Keen senses and spells
Gnome A small species born to tinker. This species has a knack for invention. Inventing
Goliath A tall species born from giants. This species is large and lives in mountains. Endurance and strength
Halfling A humble species born comforable retired. This small species is favored by luck. Luck
Human A common species born to populate. This species is good at anything they put themselves to do. Adaptability
Orc A strong species born in strife. This species is built with ultimate endurance. Survivability and adrenaline rush
Tiefling An infernal species born from lower planes. This species has devil horns but can choose to be good. Fire and dark spells
