Here we have the 16 main backgrounds in DND 5e

Background determines your characters upbringing and what skills they might have gotten before your game begins.

Background Description Abilities
Acolyte A person who has dedicated their life to serving a deity or religious cause. This background gives you access to divine magic and knowledge of religious practices. Cleric spells
Artisan A person who has mastered a craft or trade. This background gives you access to tools and knowledge of your chosen craft. Tool proficiency and craft knowledge
Charlatan A person who has lived a life of deception and trickery. This background gives you access to skills in deception and disguise. Additional skills to chose from
Criminal A person who has lived a life of crime and lawlessness. This background gives you access to skills that someone on the run might pick up. Alertness and perception
Entertainer A person who has lived a life of performance and showmanship. This background gives you access to skills in entertainment. Musical magic and support skills
Farmer A person who has lived a life of farming and rural living. This background gives you access to skills in survival and agriculture. Toughness
Guard A person who has lived a life of protecting others and maintaining order. This background gives you access to skills in defense and vigilance. Alertness and perception
Guide A person who has lived a life in the wilderness. This background gives you access to skills in navigation and survival. Druid spells
Hermit A person who has lived a life far from people. This background gives you access to skills in religion and medicine. Healing
Merchant A person who has lived a life of trade and commerce. This background gives you access to skills in negotiation and business. Luck
Noble A person who has lived a life of wealth and privilege. This background gives you access to skills in persuasion and history. Additional skills to chose from
Sage A person who has lived a life of study and research. This background gives you access to skills in knowledge and investigation. Wizard spells
Sailor A person who has lived a life at sea. This background gives you access to skills in navigation and perception. Hand to hand and improvised weapons
Scribe A person who has lived a life of writing and record keeping. This background gives you access to skills in perception and investigation. Additional skills to chose from
Soldier A person who has lived a life of military service. This background gives you access to skills in combat and athletics. Harder hitting strikes
Wayfarer A person who has lived a life of travel and exploration. This background gives you access to skills in survival and navigation. Luck
